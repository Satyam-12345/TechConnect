**TechConnect: Bridging the Digital Divide with Conversational AI**

**Concept: AI-Powered Platform for Digital Inclusion**

TechConnect is an AI-powered platform designed to make essential digital services (Travel, Healthcare, Education) accessible and intuitive for everyone, particularly those facing barriers related to digital literacy, language, and low connectivity. The core goal is to deploy an intelligent virtual assistant that simplifies complex tasks (like booking tickets or checking symptoms) into clear, step-by-step guidance, even functioning efficiently in low-bandwidth areas.
